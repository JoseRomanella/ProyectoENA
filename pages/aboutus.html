WHO

We are a nutrition, health and wellness company dedicated to the production, distribution and sale of a wide range of nutritional and health products. We seek excellence in everything we manufacture, trying to achieve the highest level and efficacy supplements, under strict quality, health and biosafety standards. Fundamentally, we focus on the quality of the products, the excellence of the raw material and the professionalism of our people. We offer the most complete line of sports supplements to improve performance, recovery and nutrition. Their objective is aimed at being able to monitor all levels of training of the different consumers, regardless of the sports activity, the type of training, the intensity or the frequency with which they do it. Our mission is to support the growth of athletes at all stages of their sports life and whatever their goals may be.

HOW

Since 1988, ENA SPORT has worked to become a leading sports supplement brand in Argentina with more than 100 product formulations, with a presence throughout the country and distribution in much of Latin America. We have a production plant equipped with state-of-the-art technology, quality analysis laboratories and the most trained personnel, working under the regulations of the National Administration of Medicines, Food and Medical Technology (ANMAT) to guarantee the safety of products when consumer. We have different agreements in force with sports institutions, nutritionists and clubs, key in the development of the sports culture of our country, which fill us with pride and reinforce our commitment to the quality and safety of our products.

QUALITY

At ENA SPORT we take the quality of our products very seriously, and to ensure that you always receive the best, each stage of the process follows the highest standards: The raw material for our proteins comes from ARLA FOODS INGREDIENTS, a world leader in its sale and production. Its quality is certified by ISO9001 and facilities registered with the FDA, to provide safety to the consumer. We maintain strategic agreements with long-term suppliers to be able to count on the latest developments in raw materials in the world. Our internal control team supervises the quality of all production processes. Every product we make is engraved with a unique tracking code to achieve traceability from the receipt of raw materials at our plant until it reaches the end consumer.

INNOVATION

We understand the importance of continuous improvement in everything we do: from product and process formulation to design and communication. For this reason, we have an interdisciplinary team that stays informed of all the latest developments, at the forefront of the category.

DEVELOPMENT

We constantly work to strengthen our work teams, reinvest, undertake and develop new products or improve existing ones, in search of excellence to accompany the consumer in the best way.
